Welcome to the Liquefaction Hazard App

Click on 'Go To Map' below to start the lookup process.

Liquefaction is loose soil that has been saturated with water that behaves like a liquid when disturbed by an earthquake. This can cause many problems to structures. To assist geotechnical engineer in identifying hazardous liquefaction areas, this app has been developed. This app provides hazardous liquefaction values include: Triggering, Lat Spread, Solid Surface Displacement, and Settlement. Values have been calculated by using the kriging or Gaussian process regression.
